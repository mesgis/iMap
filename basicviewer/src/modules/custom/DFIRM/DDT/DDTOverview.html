This tool will allow you to download data used in creating the approximate and detailed flood studies for the state of Maryland.

The data has been packaged according to Federal HUC 12 -digit watersheds.

The content for this tool is updated on an irregular time-frame, so please check back as new counties become effective. A schedule for tentative county effective dates can be found here.

Email questions or comments to mdfloodmaps@mde.state.md.us
