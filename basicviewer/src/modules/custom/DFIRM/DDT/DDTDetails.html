Find your address:

Enter an address into the "Find a place" to zoom to the closest match.

How to download the data:

Click on a location within Maryland. A popup will tell you what watershed that location is within, and a link to download the data. Download the associated zip file.

What is included in the data:

Within the zip file are two folders:

  • shapefiles
  • models

Shapefiles include:

  • Bridges/Culverts
    (NMD_bridges_culverts_shapefile)
  • Base Flood Elevation
    (BFE)
  • Tax Ditch
    (Tax_Ditches)
  • Discharge Points
    (Discharge_Points)
  • Cross Sections
    (XS_2)
  • Streams
    (Merge_S_Wtr_Ln)
  • Structure Foot Prints
    (Merge_S_Gen_Struct)
  • Coastal Barrier Resources
    (Merge_S_CBRS)
  • Hydrography
    (Merge_S_Wtr_Ar)
  • Effective FIRM Panels
    (FIRM_Panels_Effective)
  • Effective Floodplains
    (Effective_Floodplains)
  • Effective Models
    (SW_Models_Effective1)

Models folder includes hydraulic modeling data, both approximate and detailed studies, where applicable (there will be a various number depending on watershed). This folder contains the components used to complete the flood study for the designated reach in the selected watershed.
